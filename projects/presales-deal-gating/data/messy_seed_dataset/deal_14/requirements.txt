### RFP — Network & Email Security Refresh (Enterprise)
Customer: Org-14 (Manufacturing, 8,000 users)
Workstream 1 — Firewall:
- Replace Cisco ASA at 3 sites (HQ + 2 regional) with Palo Alto NGFW
- HA pair at HQ; single unit at regional sites with failover to HQ via SD-WAN
- Segmentation: OT/IT boundary at HQ plant floor (Purdue model)
- VPN for 600 remote workers
Workstream 2 — Email Security:
- Replace Symantec Email Security with Proofpoint Email Protection for 8,000 mailboxes
- M365 mail flow integration (inbound + outbound gateway mode)
- Anti-phishing, sandboxing, DMARC enforcement in scope
- Email encryption for finance and HR teams
- Log retention: 90 days for email security events
Compliance: ISO 27001 certification in progress; GDPR applies for EU customer data.
Identity: Azure AD (Entra ID) used for SSO across both workstreams.
