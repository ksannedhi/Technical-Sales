### Technical Proposal — Palo Alto Firewall Refresh

Scope: Supply, install, and commission Palo Alto NGFW at primary DC and DR site.
Deliverables:
- HA pair configuration and failover validation at both sites
- NAT and routing policy migration from Cisco ASA
- BGP peering cutover with zero downtime
- SSL inspection policy aligned to NCA ECC controls
- VPN tunnel migration (phased over 3 weekends)
- Firewall security policy baseline and hardening guide
- Acceptance test report

Timeline: 10 weeks
- Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Lab staging, policy translation, design review
- Phase 2 (Weeks 3-6): Primary DC deployment and validation
- Phase 3 (Weeks 7-9): DR site deployment and failover testing
- Phase 4 (Week 10): Documentation, handover, and sign-off

Assumptions:
- Customer provides rack space, power, and cross-connects
- Existing ASA configuration exported and shared before Phase 1
- Change management approvals obtained by customer before each maintenance window

Executive Summary:
This refresh eliminates end-of-life ASA hardware and delivers a modern NGFW platform
with centralised policy management, encrypted traffic inspection, and full NCA ECC alignment.
