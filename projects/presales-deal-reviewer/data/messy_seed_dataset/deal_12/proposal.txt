### Technical Proposal — CrowdStrike Falcon Deployment

Scope: Deploy CrowdStrike Falcon Prevent + Insight EDR across all bank endpoints and servers.
Deliverables:
- Falcon sensor deployment via GPO and SCCM
- AD group policy integration for endpoint classification
- Detection and prevention policy baselines
- Carbon Black agent removal script and validation (assumed — pending decommission schedule)
- Pilot report and go/no-go recommendation
- Executive summary and board-ready risk reduction metrics

Timeline: 12 weeks
- Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Pilot deployment on 200 endpoints, tuning
- Phase 2 (Weeks 3-8): Full workstation rollout by department
- Phase 3 (Weeks 9-11): Server rollout with change management
- Phase 4 (Week 12): Documentation, handover, CBK compliance report

Assumptions:
- Carbon Black decommission timeline to be agreed with customer IT operations
- SCCM packaging approved by customer before Phase 2
- MDR SLA terms confirmed with partner before go-live

Business value:
Replaces legacy signature-based AV with AI-driven EDR, reducing mean time to detect
from days to minutes and meeting CBK IT governance requirements.
