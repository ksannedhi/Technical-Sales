### RFP — Endpoint Protection Refresh (Regional Bank)
Customer: Org-12 (Banking)
Scope: Replace existing antivirus with CrowdStrike Falcon for all endpoints and servers.
- 3,200 Windows workstations (Windows 10/11)
- 450 Windows and Linux servers
- EDR and device control in scope; XDR integration with SIEM in phase 2
- AD integration required for policy groups and user attribution
- 90-day event retention in CrowdStrike cloud tenant
- Pilot: 200 endpoints in Week 1 before full rollout
- Existing agent: Carbon Black — decommission plan required
- No legacy OS (Windows 7 or older) in scope per customer confirmation
- Compliance: CBK IT governance framework alignment required
- SLA: critical alerts responded to within 15 minutes by MDR partner
