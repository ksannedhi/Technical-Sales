### Technical Proposal — Network & Email Security Refresh

Scope: Two parallel workstreams delivered over 16 weeks.
Deliverables:
Workstream 1 (Firewall):
- HA NGFW deployment at HQ with DR failover design
- ASA policy migration and segmentation baseline
- OT/IT boundary firewall rules (Purdue zones)
- SD-WAN integration for regional site failover

Workstream 2 (Email Security):
- Proofpoint gateway configuration for M365 mail flow
- Anti-phishing and sandbox policy tuning
- DMARC record deployment and monitoring
- Email encryption policy for sensitive departments

Timeline: 16 weeks across two workstreams running in parallel from Week 3.
Phases: Design (Wk 1-2), Deployment (Wk 3-12), Tuning and Handover (Wk 13-16).
Assumptions:
- DMARC enforcement timeline to be agreed (not yet confirmed by customer)
- OT vendor access required for Purdue boundary design — not yet scheduled
Executive summary: Eliminates two end-of-life products, closes phishing and perimeter gaps,
and advances ISO 27001 certification objectives.
